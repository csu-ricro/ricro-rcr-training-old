Obtaining Certification

PLEASE NOTE:
The Responsible Conduct of Research (RCR) Training cannot be substituted for Human Subjects Training required for individuals involved in Human Subjects Research or Laboratory Animal Use Training for individuals involved in the use of animals in their research. Please note that these are a self-paced set of modules with NO sound.

You must be a student, faculty/staff, or associate of CSU to obtain a certification.


Training Resources:
For technical assistance with the RCR Training Modules or the RCR website please contact the RCR Coordinator.
